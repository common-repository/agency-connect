Agency Connect helps agencies to improve their customer service and to increase their sales through their WordPress projects.

== Description ==

**Your service is just a click away.**

If your customer needs help while working with wordpress, you are there to support him. Right there. The request is sent straight from wordpress. No call, no text, no complicated formular. Just one click. That's service at it's best.

**The request gets to you within seconds.**

You receive an email with your customer's request for help within seconds. You can support your customer and offer your service straight away. Good for your customer and good for you. It's as easy and uncomplicated as it gets.

**Customize your Plugin.**

Of course you can customize the plugin. You can edit the texts and contact data easily and at any time.
